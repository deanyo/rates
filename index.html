betaflight rates tool

rates is for comparing setups without passing screenshots around. It supports both Actual and Betaflight, shows the graph live, and gives you a link another pilot can open directly.

what it covers

Compare roll, pitch, and yaw behavior, check throttle mid and expo, import Betaflight CLI, and export a CLI block you can paste back after choosing the right rate profile.

why use it

A shared setup opens with the same values loaded, so it is easier to compare whoop, freestyle, or race rates in Discord, Reddit, YouTube descriptions, and group chats without translating screenshots first.

scope

This is built around Betaflight first. It is not trying to cover every simulator format or every old Betaflight edge case, and it is not pretending rates are anything other than personal preference.
